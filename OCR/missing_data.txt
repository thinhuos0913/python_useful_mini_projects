Handling missing data based on proportion:
~ <1%: Safe to remove or use basic imputation (mean/median).

~ 1-5%: Remove if randomness, otherwise, use simple imputation.
~ 5-20%: Be cautious about removal: try advanced imputation

~ 20-50%: Avoid removing; use ML-based imputation methods

~ >50%: Explore alternatives, consult domain experts to avoid bias
